How to subscribe to TimesheetCreator:

If you need to hand in your timesheet every month, you can use the subscription feature of TimesheetCreator.
The details you enter into the form above will be used to generate a new timesheet every month - just like the standard timesheet generator. Near the end of the month, the document will be sent automatically to the email address provided.

The advanced section lets you specify more detailed information. However, the dates you worked will be set to some random days within the month.
